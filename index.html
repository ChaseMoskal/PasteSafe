PasteSafe
Instant Web Cryptography

Tool for instantly encrypting and decrypting text, on the fly, as you type.

Easily share sensitive information via email!

  • Press 'Encrypt', enter a password, and enter your secret message.
  • Email the link at the bottom to your friend.
  • Tell the password to your friend over the phone. Don't just send the password via email.
  • To see your message, your friend just clicks your link, and enters the password.

Open source.
Github project, MIT License.
