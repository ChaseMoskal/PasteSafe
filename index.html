pastesafe – pre-release v0.11.0
instant web cryptography

instantly encrypt text with a password as you type.

pre-release warning: code needs review!

pastesafe is a work-in-progress, and has not yet been peer reviewed by the open source community.

the security of any information used with this tool cannot be guaranteed – you may use this software at your own risk.

easily share sensitive information via email

  • press 'Encrypt', enter a password, and enter your secret message.
  • email the link at the bottom to your friend. Click and drag it right into the email (or right click it, and copy the link address).
  • tell the password to your friend verbally, in person or perhaps over the phone – don't just send the password via email.
  • to see your message, your friend just clicks your link, and enters the password.

free and open source

contributors and reviewers are welcome!

  • project on github
  • isc license

your web browser is performing the encryption

your data never leaves your computer — there is no serverside work here, just a flat HTML page hosted on GitHub. this technology is built on the web cryptography api

  • aes-256-gcm
  • 128 byte initialization vector
  • hexadecimal output

browser support is spotty

  • built for chrome
  • works in firefox (sort of)
  • internet explorer is not supported (for lack of text encoder)
